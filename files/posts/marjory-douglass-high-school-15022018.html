Marjory Douglass High School

15 February 2018

There's a real, palpable difference between "17 kids killed" and seeing picutures of the victims.

It's chilling.

Those kids could've been me. They were high schoolers with aspirations and dreams and loved ones and hopes and goals and futures ahead of them. And all of that was abruptly cut short with the quick pull of a trigger. It all...

...came

......crashing

.........down.

Life's fragile. Live it.
